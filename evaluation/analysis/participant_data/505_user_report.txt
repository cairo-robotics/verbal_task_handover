- The game has multiple rooms, lounges and hallways.
- Primary task is to talk to npc about the potions they need. Follow the instructions given at start to avoid speaking to the npc. Because that will waste time.
- Get the potions asap to the required rooms.
- Once deliver the potions you get a request to be sent to another person. Remeber the name of the receipient and the room number where you got requested. It will come in handy.

- Left path -> room 1 (gold) and room 2 (blue)
- Top path -> Left (2 npc waiting) -> top (2 npc) -> right (potion room blue and green)
- Right path -> room 3 (red) and room 4 (green)
- Bottom right -> room 5 and potion room (red and gold)
- Bottom left (2 npc)

- You send request to npc they give a response, remeber the room that made the request initially, you need to go back and deliver the response.
- Remember the name of the person who gave the response, you need that to select the right option.

- Now, upon delivering the response, you either get a treasure.
