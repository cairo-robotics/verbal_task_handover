Tutorial is basic, the game has rooms in N,S,W and E directions. (There are multiple rooms in each direction). 

1. N and S sides have NPCs to send and recieve msgs. 
2. Potions are also N and S

Adviced to go ask both rooms on W (or remeber their potion colour from the start). 
Then get one potion -> deliver, take the msg -> Give the msg -> take the response -> then new potion -> complete the msg deliver and then the portion. 

(Will reduce trips to send and recieve msgs/potions and gain more treasures)
