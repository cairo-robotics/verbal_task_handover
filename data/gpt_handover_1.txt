    "He presents with severe crushing chest pain that started 2 hours ago, radiating to his left arm and jaw. He rates the pain 8/10, associated with sweating and shortness of breath. No previous episodes reported."

Situation:

    "Currently, vital signs are BP 160/90 mmHg, HR 100 bpm, RR 18/min, and temp 98.6°F. He appears anxious and diaphoretic."

    "He's receiving oxygen via nasal cannula at 2 L/min. We've started IV access with normal saline and administered 325 mg aspirin."
